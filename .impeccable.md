# Design Context

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Hiring managers and recruiters
- **Secondary** (nice-to-have): Fellow engineers

## Use Cases

- Learn about experience and background
- Contact the site owner
- View case studies of personal projects and open source contributions
- (Future) Blog with essays on software engineering trends

## Brand Personality & Tone

- **Confident & polished** — craftsmanship matters
- No generic AI aesthetic — the current site is a point of pride
- Quality of UI/UX is a first-class concern; maintain or exceed current standard
